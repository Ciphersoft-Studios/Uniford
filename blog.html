Our certificate & online programs for 2022

We here at Eduford want to change a lot of things in 2022. The pandemic was difficult for everyone, that is why we want to create a system, where it doesn't matter if we are studying at school, at home or in a library. To archive that goal, a lot has to change. Be it the way we communicate or the way we study.


The first thing we would have to change is how we communicate with each other. If you sit in a classroom, you talk face to face with the person sitting in front of you. When you are at home, that is not an option. That is why we are switching to an online classroom tool to video chat when we are at home.


But this is just a small step in the right direction. We have to do so much more than that. But here at Eduford, we want to take on this challenge. We archive to have a fully functioning online school system at the end of 2023. While this won't be easy, it is definitely doable.
